 SN WHITE HOSTING – Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) Effective Date: February 21, 2026 1. PURPOSE This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets forth the acceptable and prohibited uses of SN WHITE HOSTING™ services, ensuring a secure, reliable, and lawful hosting environment for all clients. It applies to Shared Hosting, WordPress Hosting, Reseller Hosting, VPS, Dedicated Servers, Game Servers, StorageBox, and related services . 2. GENERAL PRINCIPLES Clients must use services in a lawful, responsible, and respectful manner , avoiding any activity that: Violates local, state, federal, or international laws Harms the security, stability, or performance of SN WHITE HOSTING infrastructure Compromises privacy or rights of other users Violation of these principles may result in immediate suspension or termination . 3.
