 SN WHITE HOSTING™ Brand Guidelines 1. Purpose These guidelines establish the proper use of SN WHITE HOSTING™ trademarks, logos, brand names, and other brand assets by affiliates, partners, resellers, and third parties. The goal is to maintain brand consistency, protect intellectual property, and ensure correct attribution in all communications. 2. Trademark Ownership SN WHITE HOSTING™, SN SOLUTIONS LLC, and related trademarks are the exclusive property of SN SOLUTIONS LLC . All rights are reserved worldwide. Unauthorized use of these marks is strictly prohibited. 3. Approved Uses of the SN WHITE HOSTING™ Brand Authorized uses of the SN WHITE HOSTING™ brand include: By authorized partners, resellers, and affiliates in promotional or marketing materials. In factual statements about services provided by SN WHITE HOSTING™. In news articles, press releases, or publications , with proper attribution. In customer testimonials or case studies , with prior permission. In educational or informational content referencing our services. 4. Logo Usage Requirements When authorized to use the SN WHITE HOSTING™ logo, the following rules apply: Use the original logo without modification. Minimum size: 100px width. Maintain clear space around the logo: at least 10px on all sides. Do not change colors, proportions, or orientation . Do not rotate, skew, or distort the logo. Ensure accompanying text is clear and readable . 5. Prohibited Uses The following uses are strictly prohibited: Modifying the logo, brand name, or tagline. Implying endorsement, partnership, or affiliation not granted by SN WHITE HOSTING™. Using the brand in misleading, offensive, or illegal contexts . Registering domain names containing SN WHITE HOSTING™ or SN SOLUTIONS . Creating confusion between your brand and SN WHITE HOSTING™. Using the brand in social media handles without permission. 6. Attribution and Credit When referencing SN WHITE HOSTING™: Use the full, proper name : "SN WHITE HOSTING™". Include the ™ symbol . Provide attribution to SN SOLUTIONS LLC . Include a link to snwhitehosting.com when possible. 7. Affiliate and Reseller Brand Use Authorized affiliates and resellers may: Use provided logos and marketing materials . Reference SN WHITE HOSTING™ in promotional content . Use the brand in product listings or descriptions . Display the SN WHITE HOSTING™ logo on their websites using approved badges. Affiliates and resellers must not create custom versions of logos or brand materials without explicit written permission . 8. Brand Material Requests To request logos, marketing materials, or approval for specific brand uses, contact: legal@snwhitehosting.com Please allow 5–7 business days for review and response. 9. Enforcement and Violations Unauthorized or improper use of SN WHITE HOSTING™ brand may result in: Cease and desist notices Content removal orders Account suspension or termination Legal action to enforce trademark rights 10. Third-Party Brand References SN WHITE HOSTING™ does not endorse or approve third-party brands, products, or services that use our name or logo without explicit written authorization. 11. Policy Amendments These guidelines may be updated at any time . Changes will be posted on snwhitehosting.com at least 15 days in advance of their effective date.
